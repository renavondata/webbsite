This matters because different annual licence fees apply based on engine size. For periods after 2016-05, click on the month to see the a breakdown by fuel and body type. If a vehicle is registered but not licensed then it is not allowed on public roads and may have already been scrapped. There are no separate data for licensed private cars by engine size.
