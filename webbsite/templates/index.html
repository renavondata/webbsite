Welcome to Webb-site Database

Emergency deployment in progress - This is a minimal viable product deployed before the October 31, 2025 shutdown of the original webb-site.com server.

Available Features

  • Search organisations - Find companies and organizations
  • Search people - Find directors, officers, and other individuals
  • Corporate events - View dividends, rights issues, and other corporate actions
  • Stock prices - View historical price charts

About This Database

The Webb-site Database contains 35 years of Hong Kong financial transparency data, including:

  • Listed companies and their officers
  • Corporate actions (dividends, rights issues, etc.)
  • CCASS beneficial ownership data
  • Directors and their relationships
  • SFC licensees and Law Society records

Data Currency

This database was imported from SQL dumps dated October 11, 2025. More features will be added after the emergency deployment.
