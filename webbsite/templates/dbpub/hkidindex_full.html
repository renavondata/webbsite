The HKID index

This index lists names, HKIDs and a link to a relevant publicly-accessible document in each case (US SEC filings, Government Gazette notices, ICAC notices, listed-company sites, etc.). ID numbers are identifiers, not secrets — they tell you virtually nothing about a person, but they let us be sure which person is meant.
