What is Renavon Hong Kong Database?

Renavon Hong Kong Database is a public financial data platform providing free access to comprehensive information about Hong Kong listed companies, corporate governance, beneficial ownership tracking through CCASS (Central Clearing and Settlement System), and related financial data spanning over 35 years.

This platform preserves and makes accessible a vast repository of Hong Kong financial market data including:

  • Company Information: Details on thousands of Hong Kong-incorporated and listed companies, including officers, advisers, ownership structures, and corporate actions
  • CCASS Holdings: Historical beneficial ownership data from Hong Kong's Central Clearing system dating back to 2007, tracking significant ownership changes
  • Stock Market Data: Historical prices, quotes, dividends, splits, and total returns calculations
  • Regulatory Data: SFC licensee information, Hong Kong Law Society records, UK Companies House data for cross-border entities
  • Corporate Governance: Board composition, committee structures, audit relationships, and adviser networks

The database is designed for researchers, investors, journalists, academics, and anyone interested in transparency and accountability in Hong Kong's financial markets.
