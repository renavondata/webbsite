Data Sources

Data is collected from multiple Hong Kong and international sources:

  • HKEX - Hong Kong Stock Exchange (stock quotes, corporate actions, CCASS beneficial ownership)
  • SFC - Securities and Futures Commission (licensee data)
  • Companies Registry - Hong Kong company incorporations and dissolutions
  • Law Society of Hong Kong - Solicitor admissions and firm positions
  • UK Companies House - UK company data (via API)
  • HKMA - Hong Kong Monetary Authority
  • Immigration Department - Passenger statistics
  • Transport Department - Vehicle registrations and traffic data

Data Coverage Timeline

  • Stock Quotes: Daily since 1994-01-03
  • CCASS Holdings: Daily since 2007-06-26 (vs. HKEX public data: only 1 year)
  • Corporate Actions: Since 1990
  • Directors & Officers: Since 1990
  • SFC Licensees: Since 2003-04-01
  • HK Solicitors: Current data with historical moves

Update Frequency

The database is updated through automated scrapers that run on various schedules:

  • Stock Quotes: Daily (evenings, after market close)
  • CCASS Data: Daily (early mornings, T+2 settlement)
  • Financial Reports: Continuous monitoring of HKEX announcements
  • SFC Licensees: Daily
  • HK Solicitors: Daily
  • Companies Registry: Weekly (when new data published)
  • UK Companies House: Continuous (via API)
